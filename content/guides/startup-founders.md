Starting a company means making high-stakes decisions with incomplete information, over and over. These mental models give founders a structured thinking toolkit for the most consequential choices they'll face.

<div style="max-width:680px;margin:var(--space-xl) auto;">

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">1</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/first-principles/">First Principles</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Break down your market, technology, and business model to fundamental truths rather than copying competitors.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">2</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/pre-mortem/">Pre Mortem</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Before launching, imagine the startup has failed and work backwards to identify the most likely causes.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">3</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/minimum-viable/">Minimum Viable</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Find the smallest possible experiment that tests your core assumption before building the full product.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">4</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/power-law/">Power Law</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">In startups, a small number of decisions produce almost all the value. Focus ruthlessly on those.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">5</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/network-effects/">Network Effects</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Understand whether your product becomes more valuable with more users — and design for it if so.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">6</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/barbell-strategy/">Barbell Strategy</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Keep enough runway to survive while making small, high-upside bets on growth experiments.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">7</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/creative-destruction/">Creative Destruction</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Understand the industry cycle you're entering: are you the disruptor or the disrupted?</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">8</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/bottleneck/">Bottleneck</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">At any given time, one constraint limits your startup's growth. Find it and fix it before optimising anything else.</p>
  </div>
</div>
