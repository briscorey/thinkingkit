The greatest investors — Buffett, Munger, Dalio, Taleb — don't just pick stocks. They use mental models to think about risk, value, and uncertainty with more discipline than the market. These frameworks form the intellectual foundation of sound investing.

<div style="max-width:680px;margin:var(--space-xl) auto;">

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">1</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/margin-of-safety/">Margin Of Safety</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Buy assets for less than they're worth. The gap between price and value is your protection against being wrong.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">2</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/circle-of-competence/">Circle Of Competence</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Only invest in businesses you genuinely understand. The edge comes from depth, not breadth.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">3</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/compounding/">Compounding</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">The most powerful force in investing. Small advantages accumulated over decades produce extraordinary results.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">4</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/probabilistic-thinking/">Probabilistic Thinking</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Think in expected values, not certainties. Every investment is a probability-weighted bet.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">5</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/loss-aversion/">Loss Aversion</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Your brain weighs losses twice as heavily as gains. This makes you sell winners too early and hold losers too long.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">6</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/antifragility/">Antifragility</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Structure your portfolio to benefit from volatility rather than being destroyed by it.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">7</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/ergodicity/">Ergodicity</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Expected value is misleading when ruin is possible. Survival comes before optimisation.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">8</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/sunk-cost-fallacy/">Sunk Cost Fallacy</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Past losses are gone. Only future expected returns matter for current decisions.</p>
  </div>
</div>
