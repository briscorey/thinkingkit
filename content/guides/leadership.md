Leading people requires understanding systems, incentives, cognitive biases, and human nature. These mental models give leaders a framework for building effective teams, making organisational decisions, and avoiding the traps that derail management.

  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/incentives/">Incentives</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">People respond to incentive structures, not speeches. Design the incentives and the behaviour follows.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">2</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/goodharts-law/">Goodharts Law</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">When a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure. Choose metrics carefully.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">3</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/dunbar-number/">Dunbar Number</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Beyond ~150 people, informal coordination breaks down. Plan organisational structure accordingly.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">4</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/peter-principle/">Peter Principle</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">People get promoted based on current performance until they reach a role they can't perform. Promote based on the skills the new role requires.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">5</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/pre-mortem/">Pre Mortem</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Before launching any initiative, give your team explicit permission to imagine failure and voice concerns.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">6</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/eisenhower-matrix/">Eisenhower Matrix</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Distinguish urgent from important. Most leadership value comes from Quadrant 2 — important but not urgent.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">7</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/reactance/">Reactance</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">People resist mandates that threaten their autonomy. Offer choices instead of commands.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">

  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/feedback-loops/">Feedback Loops</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Understand which loops drive your team's performance — and whether they're virtuous or vicious cycles.</p>
  </div>
</div>
