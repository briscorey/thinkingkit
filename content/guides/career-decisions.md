Making career decisions — whether to change jobs, ask for a promotion, start a business, or choose a specialisation — is one of the highest-stakes applications of mental models. These 8 frameworks give you a structured approach to career choices.

<div style="max-width:680px;margin:var(--space-xl) auto;">

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">1</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/regret-minimization/">Regret Minimization</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Project yourself to age 80 and ask which choice you'll regret not taking.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">2</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/optionality/">Optionality</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Prefer choices that keep doors open over those that close them permanently.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">3</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/circle-of-competence/">Circle Of Competence</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Honestly assess what you're genuinely good at versus what you wish you were good at.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">4</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/opportunity-cost/">Opportunity Cost</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">The true cost of any career choice is the best alternative you're giving up.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">5</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/antifragility/">Antifragility</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Build a career that gets stronger from uncertainty rather than being destroyed by it.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">6</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/loss-of-optionality/">Loss Of Optionality</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Distinguish reversible career moves from irreversible ones and calibrate your deliberation accordingly.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">7</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/probabilistic-thinking/">Probabilistic Thinking</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Assign probabilities to different career outcomes rather than assuming binary success or failure.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">

  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/niche-specialisation/">Niche Specialisation</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Determine whether your environment rewards specialisation or demands generalism.</p>
  </div>
</div>
