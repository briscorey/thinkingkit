Every day you make decisions — some small, some life-changing. This path gives you the specific models that improve decision quality most reliably. Follow them in order; each builds on the last.

    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Stop thinking in binary. Assign probabilities to outcomes. This is the foundation of every other decision model.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">

  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/confirmation-bias/">Confirmation Bias</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">The single most dangerous bias in decision-making. You must understand it to counteract it.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">3</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/opportunity-cost/">Opportunity Cost</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">The true cost of anything is what you give up. Every yes is an implicit no to something else.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">4</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/second-order-thinking/">Second-Order Thinking</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Think one step further. The best decisions come from asking "and then what?" before committing.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">5</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/sunk-cost-fallacy/">Sunk Cost Fallacy</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Past investments are gone. Only future value matters. This one model prevents more bad decisions than almost any other.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">6</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/inversion/">Inversion</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Before deciding what to do, figure out what would guarantee failure — then avoid those things.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">

  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/pre-mortem/">Pre-Mortem</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Imagine your decision has already failed. Work backwards to find the risks you haven't considered.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">

  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/regret-minimization/">Regret Minimization</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Project yourself to age 80. Which choice will you regret not taking? Bezos used this for the biggest decision of his career.</p>
  </div>
