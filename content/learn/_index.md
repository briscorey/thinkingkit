## Coming soon

Learning paths will guide you through mental models in curated sequences — building from foundations to advanced frameworks, with interactive exercises at each step.

**Planned paths:**

**Foundations of Better Thinking** — The 10 models everyone should know, in the order that builds understanding most efficiently.

**The Decision-Maker's Toolkit** — A focused path for people who need to make better decisions under uncertainty, combining probability thinking, bias awareness, and structured frameworks.

**Systems Thinking** — Learn to see the world as interconnected systems with feedback loops, leverage points, and emergent behaviour.

**The Argument Evaluator** — Spot logical fallacies, evaluate evidence, steelman opposing views, and update your beliefs like a Bayesian.
