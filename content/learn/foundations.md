This path takes you through the ten most foundational mental models — the ones that provide the greatest leverage across the widest range of situations. Each builds on the previous one. Follow them in order.

<div style="max-width:680px;margin:var(--space-xl) auto;">

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">1</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/map-is-not-the-territory/">The Map Is Not the Territory</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Start here. Every mental model is itself a map — a simplification of reality. Understanding this keeps you humble about all the models that follow.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">2</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/circle-of-competence/">Circle of Competence</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Know what you know and what you don't. This model is the foundation of intellectual honesty and good judgment.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">3</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/first-principles/">First Principles Thinking</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">The most powerful problem-solving tool available. Decompose any problem into its fundamental truths and rebuild from there.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">4</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/inversion/">Inversion</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">The complement to forward thinking. Ask "what would guarantee failure?" and avoid those things. Often easier than chasing success directly.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">5</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/second-order-thinking/">Second-Order Thinking</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Think one step further than everyone else. "And then what?" is the question that separates good decisions from popular ones.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">6</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/probabilistic-thinking/">Probabilistic Thinking</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Stop thinking in certainties. Assign probabilities to outcomes and make decisions based on expected value, not gut feeling.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">7</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/occams-razor/">Occam's Razor</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">The simplest explanation that fits the evidence is usually correct. A powerful filter against overthinking and conspiracy.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">8</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/hanlons-razor/">Hanlon's Razor</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Don't assume malice when incompetence or accident will explain it. Transforms how you interpret other people's behaviour.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">9</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/feedback-loops/">Feedback Loops</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">Everything is a system. Reinforcing loops amplify change; balancing loops resist it. Understanding this is the gateway to systems thinking.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="display:flex;gap:var(--space-lg);align-items:flex-start;margin-bottom:var(--space-xl);padding:var(--space-lg);background:var(--color-bg-card);border:1px solid var(--color-border-light);border-radius:var(--radius-lg);box-shadow:var(--shadow-sm);">
  <span style="font-family:var(--font-display);font-style:italic;font-size:1.8rem;color:var(--color-accent);opacity:0.4;min-width:36px;">10</span>
  <div>
    <h3 style="margin-top:0;"><a href="/models/antifragility/">Antifragility</a></h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.88rem;">The capstone. Don't just survive uncertainty — build systems that gain from it. Position yourself for upside while limiting downside.</p>
  </div>
</div>

</div>
