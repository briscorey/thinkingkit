## Why calibration matters

Well-calibrated people — those whose confidence levels match their actual accuracy — make consistently better decisions. When a calibrated person says they're "90% sure," they're right about 90% of the time. Most people are overconfident: their 90% confidence intervals contain the truth only about 50% of the time.

This trainer presents factual questions and asks you to provide a range you're 90% confident contains the answer. Over multiple questions, it measures whether your confidence matches your accuracy.
