## How to use it

Good thinking requires being able to evaluate other people's arguments — and your own — with discipline. This tool walks you through a structured analysis of any claim.

**Step 1.** Paste or type the argument you want to evaluate.

**Step 2.** Identify the core claim and the evidence offered for it.

**Step 3.** Check for common logical fallacies.

**Step 4.** Assess the strength of the evidence.
