## The model behind the tool

Inversion is one of Charlie Munger's most powerful thinking techniques: instead of asking "how do I succeed?", ask "what would guarantee failure?" — then avoid those things.

This workshop walks you through the full inversion process step by step, helping you surface risks and failure modes you might not see when thinking forward. When you're done, you'll have a concrete list of actions to take.
