## The model behind the tool

First Principles Thinking means decomposing a problem into its most basic, undeniable truths — then reasoning up from those truths to create original solutions, rather than copying what already exists.

This decomposer walks you through the full process: surface your assumptions, challenge each one, identify what's actually true, and rebuild from there.
