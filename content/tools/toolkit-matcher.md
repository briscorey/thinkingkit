## How it works

Most mental model resources give you a long list and leave you to figure out which ones apply to your situation. The Toolkit Matcher flips this: you describe what you're dealing with, and it recommends the models most likely to help.

**Step 1.** Tell it what kind of challenge you're facing — a decision, a problem to solve, a system to understand, a risk to manage, or an argument to evaluate.

**Step 2.** Add context about your specific situation using the guided prompts.

**Step 3.** Get a ranked set of recommendations with explanations of why each model fits and a direct link to learn more.

The matcher uses a curated scoring algorithm based on each model's use cases, disciplines, and situational keywords — not AI guesswork. As the model library grows, the recommendations get richer.
