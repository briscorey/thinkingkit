## Why this works

Cognitive biases operate below conscious awareness — you can't see your own blind spots by definition. A structured checklist forces you to consider each bias explicitly, which research shows reduces their influence on decision-making.

This tool covers eight of the most impactful biases. It's not exhaustive, but these eight account for the majority of decision-making errors in everyday life.
